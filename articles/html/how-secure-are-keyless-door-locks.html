Yet, as twilight creeps in, doubts about its reliability begin to cloud your mind. Did you choose the right system? Will the technology truly keep intruders at bay? These questions swirl in your thoughts as you ponder whether keyless door locks are really safe.

So, what should you consider when evaluating the safety of keyless door locks? Here are some crucial points:

  • Ensure professional installation to maximize deterrent effects.
  • Regularly maintain the system and replace batteries to avoid lockouts.
  • Use complex, unique passwords to strengthen security.
  • Secure your home Wi-Fi network to prevent unauthorized access.
  • Consider brands with proven security records, such as August and Schlage, to minimize risk.

As you weigh the pros and cons of keyless door locks, remember that while they offer a level of security and convenience, they are not infallible. Taking the necessary precautions can help you enjoy the benefits while maintaining your home’s safety.
