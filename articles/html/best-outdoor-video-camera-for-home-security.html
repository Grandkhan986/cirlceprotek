With wireless outdoor security cameras witnessing a 20% increase in adoption year-over-year, many homeowners are drawn to the convenience of wireless technology. For instance, using Wi-Fi extenders or strategically placing the camera closer to a router can make a significant difference.

Lastly, the cost can be a daunting factor. While some high-end models come with a hefty price tag, many budget-friendly options exist that still provide excellent features.
