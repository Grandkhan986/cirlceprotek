Originally developed for military use in the 1970s, the Global Positioning System was made available for civilian use in the 1990s. This marked the dawn of a new age in navigation, allowing anyone with a GPS receiver to pinpoint their location with remarkable accuracy. Samsung recognized this potential and integrated GPS capabilities into its smartphones, providing users with a powerful tool in their pockets.   • 1995: The GPS system was fully operational, leading to a surge in navigation applications.
  • 2005: The introduction of smartphones with integrated GPS transformed the way users interacted with navigation tools.
  • 2010: Major brands, including Samsung, began incorporating GPS tracking into their devices as an essential feature.
  • 2015: Samsung launched its Find My Mobile service, significantly enhancing user experience by allowing real-time tracking of devices.
