The convenience of wireless technology allows you to install and monitor your security system without the hassle of complex wiring. But what other questions should you be asking to ensure your home remains secure?

  • What smart features do I need? Many modern systems come with features like remote access, real-time alerts, and integration with home automation systems like Google Home or Amazon Alexa. Imagine being able to lock your doors or view camera feeds from your smartphone while you’re miles away.
  • How reliable is the monitoring service? Look for companies that offer 24/7 professional monitoring. Brands like ADT and Vivint have established reputations for reliability, ensuring someone is always watching out for you.
  • What about scalability? Consider whether your system can grow with you. Systems like Ring and Arlo offer modular options that allow you to add cameras or sensors as your needs change.
  • Are there hidden fees? Always read the fine print. Some companies may charge for installation, monitoring, or equipment replacement, which can affect your overall budget.
  • How does the system handle power outages? Systems with battery backups or cellular connectivity remain operational during outages, ensuring you’re never left in the dark.
  • What are the privacy implications? With increased connectivity comes greater responsibility. Understand how your data is stored and shared to protect your personal information.
  • What’s the reputation of the brand? Research customer reviews and ratings to gauge the effectiveness of the system. Brands with strong customer service, such as SimpliSafe, often have a loyal following.
