  • Check for alarms: Be cautious—some devices have hidden alarms that could trigger during removal. Use your phone to capture the area’s sound and observe for any alerts.
  • Consider professional help: If the tracker appears hardwired, seeking assistance from an automotive technician may save you time and potential complications.
