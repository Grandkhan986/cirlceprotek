Another significant hurdle lies in power management. The Raspberry Pi Zero can drain batteries quickly, especially when using a GPS module. Here are some effective solutions:

  • Utilize a power bank with a high capacity to extend usage time.
  • Implement a sleep mode in your code, allowing the Raspberry Pi to conserve energy when not actively tracking.
  • Explore solar power options for an eco-friendly solution that keeps your device charged on the go.
