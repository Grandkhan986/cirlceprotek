  • Power-Saving Modes: Many GPS apps, such as Komoot, offer low-power modes that reduce battery consumption while still providing essential navigation features.

Lastly, user error can significantly impact the effectiveness of GPS tracking. From improper settings to lack of familiarity with the technology, many users struggle to utilize GPS to its full potential. To address this, consider these tips:
