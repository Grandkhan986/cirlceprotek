So, what can you do to prevent your GPS tracker from sucking your battery dry? Here are some practical solutions to consider:

  • Optimize Settings: Adjust your GPS settings to ‘battery saver mode’ or ‘low accuracy’ when precise location tracking isn't necessary. Many devices, like those from Garmin or TomTom, offer these options.
  • Limit Background Activity: Disable GPS tracking for apps you don't frequently use. A quick check in your smartphone settings can reveal which apps are draining your battery.
  • Regularly Charge Your Vehicle: If you have a GPS tracker installed, make it a habit to drive your car at least once a week. This simple act can keep your battery healthy and fully charged.
