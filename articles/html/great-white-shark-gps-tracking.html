Consider these key milestones in the history of tracking great white sharks:

  • First Tagging Efforts (1960s): Early attempts at tracking began with simple tagging methods, providing limited data on movement.
  • Breakthrough with Satellite Technology (2000s): The implementation of GPS tracking allowed researchers to follow the journey of individual sharks in real-time, revealing their migrations of up to 2,000 miles.
  • Global Research Initiatives: Organizations like Ocearch and the Shark Research Institute have led the charge, utilizing cutting-edge technology to follow the journey of sharks around the globe.
